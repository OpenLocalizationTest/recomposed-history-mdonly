# How To: Access the MongoLab Web UI
At any time after provisioning a MongoLab database, you can access the MongoLab web UI for managing that database. We offer convenient SSO access through the Azure portal. To reach us, do the following:

1. Select **Add-ons**.  
![AddonsButton][button-addons]
1. Locate your MongoLab service in your list of add-ons.  
![MongolabEntry][entry-mongolabaddon]
1. Cick the name of your add-on to reach the add-on page.
1. Click **Manage**.  
![ManageButton][button-manage]  
You are directed
