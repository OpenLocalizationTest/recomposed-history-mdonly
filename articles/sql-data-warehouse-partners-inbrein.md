# Inbrein
![][logo]

## Who we are
[Inbrein][website] is a global IT consulting firm specialized in Microsoft Windows and Microsoft.NET platform and a Korean leader of MS BI&DW market.

We provide the total e-business solutions ranging from Information Management System to Web Business for analysis, design, consulting, and developing solutions based on our accumulated Microsoft technology, optimized consulting service for BI and DW, solutions such as SmartX.Net, iBI Framework(supporting DW standardization), MicroERD(DB modeling tool), and CDR(Clinical Data Research).

Other Inbrein services include training for professionals, operating MS BI support center, Big and Data presentation.
