# Customer case studies
Below are a collection of customers who have used Azure Service Fabric to build services that run on Azure today and their stories of why Service Fabric was the right technology for them to use.

# OSISoft : operational intelligence
OSISoft is a global leader in operational intelligence. To provide the scalability needed to meet the exponential growth in the volume of data collected and stored, OSIsoft enhanced the PI System with cloud-scale capabilities using Microsoft Azure. [Read here][1] on the details of how they used Service Fabric to transform their existing business.

# Talk Talk with blinkbox : media content provider
Based in the UK, blinkbox, a TalkTalk company, delivers the newest blockbusters on demand months before they're available on subscription services. With continued rapid growth and new business requirements blinkbox used a microservices-based approach to their application design using Service Fabric. [Read here](service-fabric-customer-blinkbox.md) on the details of their design.
 
# Schneider Electric : energy management and automation
Schneider is a global specialist in energy management and automation. The challenge for Schneider was to reduce the development and delivery cost, while providing flexibility and extensibility for customization of specific applications that serve the vertical markets.  To fulfill this challenge, Schneider chose to leverage Azure Service Fabric to build an ‘Extensible Platform for Management & Operation of Devices’ which was both flexible and scalable enough to scale to millions of devices for both telemetry and command with millisecond response rates.[Read here](service-fabric-customer-blinkbox.md) for the details of their design.

# Microsoft Cortana : personal digital assistant
Building Bing Cortana as an always-on, hyperscale cloud service. [Read here][4] on how this Microsoft development team, responsible for building the Cortana personal digital assistant, faced a big challenge and used Service Fabric as the solution.
