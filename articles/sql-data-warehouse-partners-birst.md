#Birst
![][logo]
##Who we are
[Birst][website] is the global leader in Cloud BI and Analytics. The company helps organizations make thousands of decisions better, every day, for every person. Birst enables enterprises to create a trusted source of data, place it in the context of key business users and then enable business users up and down the organization to report and analyze the information using world-class BI tools. Thousands of the most demanding businesses trust Birst to make metric-driven business execution a reality. 
The general problem that Birst addresses is the access to all of the necessary date within an organization in a manner which is analytic ready so that users can make informed decisions about their area of the business.  This process also needs to address the issues of speed, agility, scalabilty, security and governance of the data so that users do not have to wait to make important decisions due to IT needing to rewrangle the data for every new analytic request.

##Solution introduction
Birst patented a 2-tier data architecture and comprehensive BI platform sits on top of all of your data, to unify, refine and automate the user data tier in SQL databases on Azure. The data can then be embedded consistently into every individual decision-up and down the org chart. In addition, Birst can be supported in an Azure private cloud via its appliance deployment capabilities. 

Highlights:

•	Automated data refinement: Rapidly deploy and adapt to changes in requirements. Reduce administrative costs. Get quick answers to your next questions.

•	Complete, End-to-End Cloud Platform: Gain insight through a single, integrated solution that makes data analytic ready and visualizes it via dashboards, ad-hoc, pixel-perfect reports, and mobile.

•	Integrated Logical Layer: Address hard business questions, fast. Empower business users to quickly unlock the analytic possibilities in their data.
