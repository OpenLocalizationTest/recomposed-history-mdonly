# Azure Government Marketplace
For partners interested in publishing your offerings to the Azure Marketplace for Government, find the details in this article.

## Publishing
> [!NOTE]
> If you are not an existing Azure Certified Marketplace partner, complete the steps that show how to [publish and manage an offer](../marketplace-publishing/marketplace-publishing-getting-started.md) before proceeding.
>
>

### Step 1
Sign in to [https://publish.windowsazure.com](https://publish.windowsazure.com).

### Step 2
Click the offer that you want to publish.

### Step 3
Click **SKUS**, and click the **Azure Government Cloud** box.

> [!NOTE]
> Only Bring Your Own License (BYOL) SKUs are supported.  This option is not available for Pay-as-You-Go (PayG) SKUs.
>
>

![Screenshot of the offer page. Shows where the SKUS and Azure Government Cloud options are located](./media/government-manage-marketplace-partner-1.png)

### Step 4
Click the **+ Add Certification** link to add links to any certifications for your offer.

![Screenshot of the offer page. Shows where to locate the Add Certification link](./media/government-manage-marketplace-partner-2.png)

### Step 5
To test your image in the publishing portal, request a trial account in Microsoft Azure Government cloud: [https://azuregov.microsoft.com/trial/azuregovtrial](https://azuregov.microsoft.com/trial/azuregovtrial).

Your eligibility as a partner who serves US federal, state, local, or tribal entities are verified and confirmation will be provided via email.  Your trial account will be available in 3 to 5 business days.

### Step 6
Click **Publish**, and click **Push to Staging**.

![Screenshot that shows the Publish and Push to Staging options](./media/government-manage-marketplace-partner-3.png)

You will be prompted to enter a whitelisted subscription that has access to the staged offer. Enter the subscription ID from your newly acquired Azure Government trial account.

![Screenshot where you specify the subscription IDs that can access the offer](./media/government-manage-marketplace-partner-4.png)

### Step 7
After the offer is successfully staged, you can test your image by signing in to [https://portal.azure.us](https://portal.azure.us) using your Azure Government trial account.

### Step 8
After you have validated your image by using the trial subscription, you can make the offer available live by clicking **Publish** and requesting approval to go to production.

![Screenshot that shows the request approval to go to production command](./media/government-manage-marketplace-partner-5.png)
